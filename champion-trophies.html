CHAMPION TROPHIES & AWARDS

Champion Trophies & Awards is a small, family-owned business who specializes in engraving and building custom awards.

Visit Site

BRIEF

Create an original brand identity for Champion Trophies & Awards that can unify the visual language of all the entire business. This includes a logo, icons, a color palette, website, business cards, and flyers.

OBJECTIVE

Create an identity for Champion Trophies & Awards that reflects both the engraving industry and the family-owned/local aspect of the business, which has been a part of the Morgantown community for about forty years.

SOLUTION

The solution that I arrived to was to create a brand that incorporated elements of typographic flourishes and ornamentation as those were often used on the business' hand-engraved work in previous decades in combination with a sharp, flat-plane logo and set of icons. These components are meant to lead the viewer to identify Champion Trophies & Awards as a family-owned, local shop whose decades of experience continues to grow and evolve with both the industry and the community. The logo itself contains a trophy symbol, and the traditional seal also doubles as a medalion.
