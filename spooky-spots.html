SPOOKY SPOTS OF WEST VIRGINIA

Spooky Spots of West Virginia is an online travel guide for urban explorers and uncoventional tourists.

Visit Site

BRIEF

Design a map for urban explorers in West Virginia. It can exist in any form, web, print, etc.

OBJECTIVE

Introduce urban explorers to a number exciting places to investigate in West Virginia, as well as properly prepare them for their adventure.

SOLUTION

My solution is the multi-platform service, Spooky Spots of West Virginia, begins with a website that has a game-like experience where users use a flashlight to explore the Mountain State for spooky attraction. (Users can skip the flashlight search by turning on the lights). Once a site of interest is found, you can access a quick history of the spot, materials to bring, and risk factors involved. Finally, you can download a pint-and-fold-yourself zine as a pocket guide for your adventure.
