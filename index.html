ABOUT ME

My name is Zane Huggins. I am a graphic designer who believes in designing for a purpose, designing for a need, and designing for a cause. I am an evolving artist who finds beauty in the uncommon places of the world. I focus on branding and identity development including, but not limited to, logos, iconography, web design, and stationary design. I am a senior Graphic Design student at West Virginia University. I will be graduating in May of 2020.
