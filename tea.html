THE TEA

The Tea is a publication made for coffee shop reading. Each volume covers a topic meant to pick the readers brain and spread important information with a friendly dimeanor.

BRIEF

Design a publication and volume covering the topic of technological waste. The publication should use infographics and photos to engage readers.

OBJECTIVE

Spread important and often overlooked information to coffee shop visitors in an interesting and easily consumable publication.

SOLUTION

A four-fold front and back publication using typographic treatment to create metaphors and friendly colors and icons to keep the publication friendly despite the seriousness of the volume's topic.
